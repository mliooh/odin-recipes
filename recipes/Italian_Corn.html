Italian Corn

This is a delicious seasoned corn side dish.

Ingridients

  • 1 ½ tablespoons butter
  • 2 ½ cups whole kernel sweet corn
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over high heat. Heat butter in hot skillet until almost completely melted; stir in corn. Seasoncorn with Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Cook seasoned corn until light golden brown, about 5 minutes.
